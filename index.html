Context

Generali

Working with you since 1831

Generali is one of the largest global insurance and asset management providers. Established in 1831, it is present in 50 countries in the world, with a total premium income of more than € 69.7 billion in 2019. With nearly 72,000 employees serving 61 million customers, the Group has a leading position in Europe and a growing presence in Asia and Latin America. Generali’s ambition is to be the life-time partner to its customers, offering innovative and personalized solutions thanks to an unmatched distribution network.


‍My missions are complementary continuation of a research that was conducted by Ms. Cassandre Hutin with our mentor Mr. Giovanni Ricciolio as part of Digital Strategy and Acceleration team at Generali Investments. She was led to develop an issue related to digital technology adapted to the Asset Management sector, more precisely UX, SEO and the machine learning.


Her objectives were to learn about the Asset Management industry, discover the scope of Machine Learning and its impact in SEO, set up a demonstration and finally apply the knowledge gained to a concrete case. I’m Khalil Abi and my goal for this project is to follow up on this research and put in place a plan for practical exploration of the solutions previously proposed.
